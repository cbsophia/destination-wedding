Welcome

We are so happy you'll be joining us in Peru to celebrate our wedding.

If you had told Marije years ago that she would fall in love with a Peruvian, get married in Peru, and convince family and friends from around the world to travel to the Sacred Valley for a wedding weekend, she probably wouldn't have believed you. Yet here we are.

This trip will be especially meaningful because, although Marije visited Peru before we met, it will be our first time experiencing it together. We can't wait to share this beautiful place — and this special moment in our lives — with all of you.

On this website you'll find everything you need to know for the wedding weekend, along with travel tips, recommendations, and inspiration for exploring Peru. Whether you're joining us just for a few days or turning this into a longer adventure, we hope you'll discover some of the places, people, and experiences that make Peru so special to us.

With love,
Marije & Sophia

Explore Peru

For many of our guests, the wedding will be part of a larger adventure through Peru. We've gathered a few ideas to help you decide where to spend your time before or after the wedding.

Lima

Peru's capital is known for its incredible food scene, coastal views, museums, and lively neighborhoods.

Cusco

Explore Cusco City, the Sacred Valley, and Machu Picchu — the heart of Peru's Andean region and the setting for our wedding weekend.

Other Destinations

Arequipa, Huaraz, Ica, Puerto Maldonado, Piura, and Puno are all wonderful options if you want to discover more of Peru.
