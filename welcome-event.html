Welcome Event

We couldn't imagine a better way to begin our wedding weekend than by welcoming you to the mountains of the Sacred Valley.

Surrounded by breathtaking views and the natural beauty of the Andes, we'll spend the evening celebrating with the people we love most. The setting is outdoors and intentionally relaxed, allowing everyone to settle in, reconnect, and soak in the magic of Peru before the wedding day.

As the evening unfolds, temperatures may cool, so we recommend bringing a jacket, shawl, or extra layer.

Dress Code

Mountain chic. Think relaxed elegance with an adventurous spirit.

The Look

  • Earthy tones and natural textures
  • Linen, suede, wool, denim, leather details, and knitwear
  • Boots, loafers, flats, or comfortable shoes
  • Layers encouraged for the evening
