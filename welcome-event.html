Welcome Event

Date

Friday, 18 June 2027

Location

Surprise mountain setting

Transportation

Provided from the hotel

Dress Code

Mountain Chic

We couldn't imagine a better way to begin our wedding weekend than by welcoming you to the mountains of the Sacred Valley.

Surrounded by breathtaking views and the natural beauty of the Andes, we'll spend the evening celebrating with the people we love most. The setting is outdoors and intentionally relaxed, allowing everyone to settle in, reconnect, and soak in the magic of Peru before the wedding day.

As the evening unfolds, temperatures may cool, so we recommend bringing a jacket, shawl, or extra layer.

"An evening in the Andes with our favorite people."

Transportation

Transportation will be provided for all guests staying at Sonesta Posadas del Inca Yucay.

Guests are kindly requested to gather in front of the hotel by 15:15 for departure. The journey to the location takes approximately 25 minutes.

Return transportation to the hotel will also be provided at the end of the evening.

Dress Code

Mountain chic. Think relaxed elegance with an adventurous spirit. Think of the following color palette:

The Look

  • Earthy tones and natural textures
  • Linen, suede, wool, denim, leather details, and knitwear
  • Boots, loafers, flats, or comfortable shoes
  • Layers encouraged for the evening

Practical Tips

Warm Layers

Evenings in the mountains can cool down quickly after sunset.

Outdoor Setting

Celebrate beneath open skies and spectacular Andean views.

Comfortable Footwear

Choose shoes that allow you to move comfortably throughout the evening.
